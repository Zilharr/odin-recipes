Description

Succulent pork shanks with irresistible crackling, spiced and oven-roasted for
three hours and served with sauerkraut and mashed potatoes. Mmmmm.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp dried marjoram
  • 2 tbsp dried basil
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 tbsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp vegetable bouillon powder
  • 6 (1 1/2) lbs pork shanks

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Whisk olive oil, marjoram, basil, rosemary, thyme, sea salt, paprika, black
    pepper, and vegetable bouillon powder together in a bowl. Rub pork shanks
    with herb rub and arrange on a large baking pan.
  3. Roast in preheated oven until shanks are tender and outer skin is crispy, about 3 hours.
